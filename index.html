About Us

The system uses RFID cards and big data technology to give users a traceable and safe experience.

Motivation

The effort is motivated by multiple reports of serious occurrences, including assault and harassment, occurring on Indian public transportation. The project team is committed to tackling these issues and coming up with a solution that will increase everyone's access to secure and convenient public transit. The project has the potential to significantly affect the security of public transportation.

The prototype will show how well big data technology and RFID cards work together to guarantee traceability and security for public transportation users. The long-term goal is to design a system that not only protects users but also fosters trust in public transportation as a dependable and safe form of transportation.

Why Choose Us

Security

The security model ensures the safety of people using public transportation by combining RFID card technology with real-time monitoring to enable traceability, prompt emergency response, and proactive incident detection.

Cost effective

As a result of the financial strength of our solution, users and transportation providers will not have to sacrifice affordability to get the benefits of increased security.

Alerts

This system sent alert especially for school childern when vehicle is overloaded.

Happy passengers

Passengers like this model because it gives them a real sense of security, making it possible for them to travel on public transit with assurance that their movements are tracked, emergency help is close at hand, and incidents are handled before they become serious problems. This improves the customers' overall travel experience.
